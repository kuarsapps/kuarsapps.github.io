Jarmanima visvako sabaibhanda purano rastriya sosala heltha insyorensa pranali ho juna san 1883 ko oto vona bismirkako rogasilata bima kanunako puna: Miti suru bha'eko cha. Halako 85% abadi eka adharabhuta svasthya bima yojanale karagaradvara pradana gareko cha, jasale kavarejako manaka stara pradana gardacha. . Sesa niji svasthya bima ko lagi cunachana, jo aksara atirikta labha pradana gardacha. Visva svasthya sangathanako anusara, jarmaniko svasthya heravicara pranali 77% sarakari sarakari ra vitta posita thiyo ra 23% niji rupama 2004 ko rupama vitta posita bhayo. Sarakara ansika rupama kama majaduri karmacariharuko lagata phirta garcha, jasako primiyama purvanirdharita mulyama chadi'eko cha. Ucca majadura karmacariharule uniharuko vetanako adharama eka primiyama bhuktani garchan. Uniharule niji bimako lagi pani rojna sakdacha juna saman'yataya adhika mahangi cha, tara jasako mulya vyaktigata svasthyako sthitima adharita huna sakcha. Pratiphala sulka-seva-adharita adharama cha, tara cikitsakaharuko sankhya eka di'i'eko sthaniyama vaidhanika svasthya bimala'i anumati di'i'eko cha sarakari ra vyavasayika samaja dvara viniyamita. Asuransi Kesehatan Terbaik Dan Murah Sahakaryako prayoga garna ko lagi sahakarmila'i 1 9 80 ko dasakama pesa gariyo. Jarmanima aspatala basnako ausata lamba'i hala 14 varsadekhi 9 dinasam'ma ghatyo, sanyukta rajya amerika (5 dekhi 6 dina) sam'mako ausatama ajhai dherai bhanda badhi lamo cha. Antara ko bhaga yo cha ki aspatala ko pratiphala ko lagi mukhya vicara aspatala ya dinom ko sankhya ko rupa ma prakriya ya nidana ko virodha ho. Ausadhiko lagata badhdo cha, 1 99 1 dekhi 2005 sam'ma lagabhaga 60% badhdai ga'eko cha. Lagata samavesa garne prayasako bavajuda, samagra svasthya heravicara vyatita 2005 ma jidipiko 10.7% Guna badhyo, an'ya pascimi yuropiya rastraharuko tulanama, tara amerikama kharca bhanda kama (sakala gharelu utpada ko lagabhaga 16%).
