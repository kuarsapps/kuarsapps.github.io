Perihal Piala Dunia 2018 Kuitanitsa njira zowonjezera makapu a World FIFA 2018 ndi 2022 anayamba mu Januwale 2009, ndipo mayiko a mayiko akhalapo mpaka 2 February 2009 kulembetsa chidwi chawo. Poyamba, mayiko asanu ndi anayi adayitanitsa makapu a World Cup Cup 2018, koma Mexico adachokapo, ndipo dziko la Indonesia linakanidwa ndi FIFA mu February 2010 boma la Indonesia litalephera kulemba kalata yothandizira. Panthawiyi, mayiko atatu otsala omwe si a UEFA (Australia, Japan, ndi United States) adachoka pang'onopang'ono kuchokera mu 2018, ndipo mayiko a UEFA adatsutsidwa pa 2022. Momwemonso, pamapeto pake panali mayina anayi a mu 1988 FIFA ya World Cup: England, Russia, Netherlands / Belgium, ndi Portugal / Spain. Komiti Yaikulu ya FIFA ya makumi awiri ndi ziwiri inasonkhana ku Zurich pa 2 December 2010 kuti ivotere kusankha masewera awiriwo. Russia idapatsidwa ufulu wokhala nawo 2018 pambali yachiwiri ya kuvota. Kutsatsa kwa Portugal / Spain kunabwera kachiwiri, ndipo kuchokera ku Belgium / Netherlands katatu.
